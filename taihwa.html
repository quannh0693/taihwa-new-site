About Us

IF YOU REALLY WANT TO KNOW THE DELICIOUSNESS, HAVE SOME NOODLE

Tai Hwa Pork Noodle was started by our founder Mr Tang Joon Teo in 1932 at Hill Street. When the late Mr Tang passed away, his sons continued the business. In 1990s, the shop moved to Marina Square. In 2004 it moved again to its current location at Crawford Lane. Note that this is THE ONE AND ONLY OUTLET for Hill Street Tai Hwa Pork Noodle. All the other outlets you see in food courts do not belong to us. If you are a bak chor mee fan and has never try this before, you are definitely missing out. We look forward to serving you soon! Don't forget to follow us at taihwa.com.sg
